Company Name: Core Afrique Investment Ltd

Description:
Core Afrique Investment Ltd (CAI) is a Ghana-based investment and advisory firm committed to building future-ready enterprises and informed markets.

Core Afrique Investment Ltd is a Ghanaian boutique investment and advisory firm focused on unlocking value across emerging technologies, digital assets, and frontier markets. With deep expertise in Blockchain, virtual assets, and capital formation, CAI operates at the intersection of innovation, regulation, and sustainable economic growth in Africa.

Overview:
Under Ghana's Virtual Asset Service Provider (VASP) framework, CAI operates within the Advocacy category, focusing on education, literacy, and public awareness rather than custodial or transactional services.

Our programs support Ghana's National Virtual Assets Literacy Initiative (NaVALI) by promoting informed, responsible, and compliant adoption of Blockchain technology.

As part of Ghana's National Virtual Assets Literacy Initiative (NaVALI) ecosystem, CAI delivers:
- Blockchain and digital asset education
- Public and private sector training
- Policy-aligned advocacy and literacy programs
- VASP compliance education

Role: Founder & Principal Advisor
Email: frank@coreafrique.com

Biography:
Frank Tawiah is the Founder and Principal Advisor of Core Afrique Investment Ltd (CAI). He is a Knowledge Partner for Ghana's National Virtual Assets Literacy Initiative (NaVALI) under the country's VASP regulatory framework, bringing over eight years of experience in Blockchain technology, digital assets, and decentralized systems.

His work spans education, advisory, ecosystem development, and policy engagement, positioning CAI as a trusted bridge between innovation and regulation in Africa's emerging digital economy.

Frank is a Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) and holds an MBA in Finance and Investments from the Schulich School of Business, York University, Canada. He has performed due diligence and investment valuation on numerous projects across extractive industries, infrastructure, telecommunications, and digital assets in Ghana and Africa.

As the lead consultant for Core Afrique Investment Ltd., Frank has carried out valuations of unlisted investments for major institutions including SSNIT, Ghana's public pension fund. He has authored business plans, information memoranda, and investment documents for investors, corporates, and emerging technology ventures.

Beyond CAI, Frank serves as a director of multiple companies and has been an instructor for the Ghana Stock Exchange Securities Course and an adjunct lecturer at the Ghana Institute of Management and Public Administration (GIMPA). He has high working proficiency in French (Diplôme de Langue, Alliance Française).
